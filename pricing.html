Formosan Black Bear

The Formosan black bears are representative animals in Taiwan and endemic subspecies. These solitary animals have several nicknames, such as dog bear and moon bear. I will introduce several things about Formosan black bears, such as appearance, diet, and unique behavior. The first is the appearance, they have round heads, big round ears, they look like Mickey Mouse's ears. They also have dog-like noses, meaning they have a good sense of smell, and long hair around their short necks. Like us, Formosan black bears have five-toed feet, and their most iconic feature is the V-shaped white marks on their chest. Then comes the diet. Formosan black bears are classified as carnivores, but more than 80 percent of their diet are plant-based and they are omnivores. Finally is their uniqueness, because Taiwan has a warm climate and their food supply is sufficient, Formosan black bears don't hibernate. Also, they are the only bears in the world that build nests. Besides that, they are also very good at climbing trees, so if you see them in the wild, don't think climbing a tree will be safe.
