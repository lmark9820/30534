Mikado Pheasant

The exhibit of Mikado Pheasant is in Taiwan's Ecology in the Global Environment Hall.They have some different features between male and female. The males have dark blue plumage and white stripson wings and tails. The females have brown plumage to camouflage in the habitat and females are smaller and lighter than males.Mikado Pheasants live in high elevation mountain areas in Taiwan like the Central Mountain Range and Snow Mountain. They often appear at dense shrubs,brush and steep slopes.They are easily seen at dawn and dusk. They not only like rainy and foggy weather, but also love staying in the mist. One of the most known features of Mikado Pheasant can be found on Taiwan's NT1000 dollar banknotes.



Brochure And Introduction Videos

For this exhibit, we not only made a trifold brochure, but also made a video. When making the three-fold pages, I was in charge of taking pictures. In the production part of the film, I was in charge of directing and reviewing scripts. This assignment really tested us. We encountered many emergencies. The scheduled shooting time just overlapped with the maintenance time of the exhibits, which made us unable to record in the original place. Fortunately, there is another specimen in the Museum of Science and Technology. On the day of shooting, we also found a mural of Mikado Pheasant, which allowed us to add a scene that can be photographed. I was mainly in charge of the habitat part, starting from the 57th second of the film is my commentary.
