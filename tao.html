Tao Tribe

There is a group of aborigines living on Lanyu, known as the Tao people. Because of the climate, the Tao people will have cold northeast monsoons in winter and typhoons in summer, so they build their houses underground. They make a living by fishing, and their boats are called plank boats, and three kinds of totems can often be seen on the boats. The first is human figures symbolizing their ancestors,who will protect them.Second, the concentric circle, which looks like the sun, serves as the eye of the boat.Tao people believe this totem will help them find fish groups. At last, the wave totem are the representations of the ocean waves. Before launching the plank boats, Tao people usually have a ceremony. The men are responsible for holding the boat. The women are usually responsible for growing taro roots, so they will put a lot of taro roots inside the boat to symbolize that they could have a good harvest.
